\\\ The Fries


  • ➡ Two baths for perfect Fries

    Cut the Potatoes | into 1 to 1.5 cm slices. Stack these slices and cut them into the same width sticks to make matchstick fries

    Soak the Potatoes | for at least 20 minutes or up to an hour in order to get rid of excess starch, resulting in fluffier and crispier fries.

    Drain and Dry the Potatoes | arrange them in a single layer to air-dry for a bit. The drier they are before frying, the better

    Fry the Potatoes | bring at least 5 cm of oil in a large heavy pot to 160 C. Once the oil is hot, fry the potatoes for about 5 minutes

    Drain and Cool the Half-Done Fries | drain them on a cooling rack. Let sit until completely cool

    Fry the Potatoes Again | bring the oil up to the 175 C to 190 C range.Add the fries and fry for 5 minutes until golden

    Drain the Fries| drain them on a cooling rack. If you're salting your fries, season them while they are draining and still hot

    Serve and Enjoy
