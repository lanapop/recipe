Carbonara Pasta recipe


Start with the pastas

  • Mix together 100 grams of 00 flour to 100 grams of semolina
  • Add two egg yolks
  • Add 1 large spoon of oliv oil and 5 grams of salt
  • Blend it all together and work the dough until not sticky
  • Shape the dough in a bowl and put it in the fridge for an hour
  • Once the dough is ready to be used, rework the dough with your hands a couple of minutes and then use the laminoir to make spaghettis

Ingredients

  • 100 grams of OO flour
  • 100 grams of semolina flour
  • 2 large fresh eggs
  • 1 large spoon of olive oil
  • 5 grams of sea salt




Then prepare the ingredients for the sauce

  • Cut the Guanciale in 2 cm cubes, put them in a pan and fry them until crispy
  • Once crispy, put the fat in a bol you would use later
  • Grate the pecorino in a separate bowl
  • Put eggs yolks in a large bowl and stir them with the grated pecorino and the pepper. Steam in the guanciale fat and continue to stir until combined

Ingredients per person

  • 1 egg yolk
  • 3 spoons of grated pecorino romano
  • 12 cubes of Guanciale
  • 100 grams of pasta
  • 1 spoon of black pepper

Assemble your dish

  • Add the pastas in boiling waters. As they are fresh, only a couple of minutes are required. Do not overcook them!
  • Once the pastas are al dente, add them into the bowl with the yolks and pecorino
  • Use tongs to stir the pastas with the sauce
  • Add a couple spoons of water from the pastas to make a creamy texture. Do not add too much water, the spagghetis should only be coatted with the sauce and not immersed in it!
