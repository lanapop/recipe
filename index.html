Carbonara Pasta

Ingredients

The quality of the ingredients is key to a perfect pasta dish

  • Pastas
  • Eggs
  • Pecorino Romano
  • Water

Steps

Make the spaghettis

  1. Mix together 100 grams of 00 flour to 100 grams of semolina
  2. Add two egg yolks

Cook the pastas

Add the egg yolks and the pecorino romano
