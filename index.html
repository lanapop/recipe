Spaghetti alla Carbonara


STEP 1 | Start with the pastas

  • Mix together the flour and the semolina. Use your fingers to create a good-sized well in the middle of the flour mound
  • Add the eggs in the center of the well
  • Add the olive oil and the salt
  • Blend it all together and knead the dough until it is smooth and elastic, sprinkling some extra flour on the cutting board if the dough seems too wet or sticky
  • Form the dough into a ball, wrap it with a plastic film and put it in the fridge for an hour
  • Once the dough is ready to be used, rework it with your hands a couple of minutes and then use a pasta maker to make spaghettis

Ingredients

  • 100 gr of OO flour
  • 100 gra of semolina flour
  • 2 large fresh eggs
  • 1 large spoon of olive oil
  • 5 gr of sea salt



STEP 2 | Prepare the ingredients for the sauce

  • Cut the Guanciale in 2 cm cubes, put them in a pan and fry them until crispy
  • Once crispy, put the fat in a bowl - for later use
  • Grate the pecorino in a separate bowl
  • Put eggs yolks in a large bowl and stir them with the grated pecorino and the pepper. Steam in the guanciale fat and continue to stir until combined

Ingredients per person

  • 1 egg yolk
  • 3 spoons of grated pecorino romano
  • 12 cubes of Guanciale
  • 100 grams of pasta
  • 1 spoon of black pepper

STEP 3 | Assemble your dish

  • Add the pastas in boiling water. As they are freshly made, only a couple of minutes are sufficient!
  • Once the pastas are Al dente, add them into the bowl with the yolks and pecorino
  • Use tongs to stir the pastas and the sauce
  • Add a couple spoons of water from the pastas to make a creamy texture. Do not add too much water, the spagghetis should only be coatted with the sauce and not immersed in it!
